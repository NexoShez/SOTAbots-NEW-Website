SOUTH SOUND SCRIMMAGE 2025!

Our Week 0 Event is on its way!
The South Sound Scrimmage is an on-season event run by the Tacoma Robotics Alliance and The SOTAbots, Started in the 2024-2025 season FIRST® DIVE℠ presented by Qualcomm®, 10 teams showed up and competed in 15 matches at our practice field. It was broadcasted on the SOTAbots Youtube channel on February 23rd.


This years scrimmage is much more "planned" than last years, with a redone logo for both standard graphics and for themed graphics. It is planned to have better stream quality aswell.

We hope to see you there!
