HOW YOU CAN HELP

SOTAbots Team Member Fees

The SOTAbots have fees for various events or supplies.

TEAM FEE

$50

If you donate $50, you will fund one student's team fee into the team, which gives them a team shirt and safety glasses.

DISTRICTS

$200-$500

If you donate $200-$500, you'll fund one student's travel fee to go to District Events & Championship, which will include room and board for the time we're there.

WORLDS

$2000

If you donate $2000, you can fund one student's travel fee to go to World Championships in Houston, which will include airfare, room, and board when we're there.

Through Elements of Education

The SOTAbots is part of the non-profit organization - Elements of Education Partners. We appreciate both in kind and monetary donations and sponsorships. A procurement form can be provided on request. The tax ID # for Elements is #91-2168988. Checks should be made payable to "Elements of Education" with "SOTAbots" on the memo line. ALL donations of money and physical items are fully tax deductible and very appreciated.
